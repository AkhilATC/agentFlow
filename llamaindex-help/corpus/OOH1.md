## Out-of-Home (OOH) Marketing Definition

Out-of-Home (OOH) marketing refers to advertising that reaches consumers outside their homes in public spaces. It includes static and digital media formats placed in high-footfall areas such as roads, transit hubs, malls, and urban centers.

### Common OOH Formats
- Billboards and hoardings
- Transit media such as buses, taxis, metro stations, and airports
- Street furniture including kiosks and bus shelters
- Retail signage and storefront branding
- Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH) LED screens
- Ambient and experiential installations

### Objectives of OOH Marketing
- Increase brand awareness and recall
- Maintain high-frequency exposure
- Target geographic locations and demographics
- Drive offline retail footfall
- Position brands as premium or mass-market
