## Kerala Marketing Landscape Overview

Kerala is a highly literate and media-consuming state in India with strong regional language media influence. Consumers show high engagement with newspapers, television, and digital platforms.

### Key Market Characteristics
- High literacy rate and digital adoption
- Strong regional language consumption (Malayalam)
- Festival-driven purchasing behavior (Onam, Vishu, Ramadan, Christmas)
- Tourism-driven economy contributing to advertising demand
