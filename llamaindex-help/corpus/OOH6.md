## OOH Advertising Use Cases

### Retail and Consumer Goods
Jewellery, textiles, electronics, and FMCG brands use OOH for brand visibility and festival promotions.

### Automotive
Car dealerships and two-wheeler brands use hoardings for product launches and offers.

### Tourism and Hospitality
Resorts, airlines, and travel agencies advertise in airports, highways, and tourist zones.

### Education and Healthcare
Coaching institutes and hospitals use OOH for local brand awareness and trust-building.
