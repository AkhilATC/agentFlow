## Consumer Behavior in Kerala

Kerala consumers respond strongly to regional language content and culturally relevant storytelling.

### Behavioral Patterns
- Preference for Malayalam creatives over English-only campaigns
- High trust in local brands and purpose-driven marketing
- Sustainability and eco-friendly branding influence purchase decisions
- Strong seasonal demand during festivals and tourism seasons
