## Digital Marketing Trends in Kerala

Digital platforms account for a growing share of advertising budgets in Kerala, with video and social media dominating consumption patterns.

### Key Trends
- High engagement with short-form video content (Reels, Shorts)
- Increasing use of AI tools for content and ad automation
- Growth of voice search in Malayalam language
- Omni-channel marketing across Google, Meta, YouTube, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn
- Social commerce adoption by small businesses and artisans
