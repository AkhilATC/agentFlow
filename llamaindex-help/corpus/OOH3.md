## OOH Advertising Trends in Kerala

OOH advertising in Kerala is dominated by highways, airports, malls, and urban centers such as Kochi, Thiruvananthapuram, and Kozhikode.

### Major Trends
1. Retail-driven hoarding campaigns during festivals
2. Highway and transit branding due to tourism and commuter traffic
3. Growth of Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH) screens in malls and city centers
4. Hyperlocal district-level targeting using regional language creatives

### Key OOH Locations
- National highways (NH66)
- Kochi Metro stations
- Airports and railway stations
- Shopping malls and commercial streets
